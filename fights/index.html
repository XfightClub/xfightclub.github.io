Fights!

Fight Info
We've got the blood, guts and butts, now that we got your butt in the ring here are the rules!

We start calling for fighters on the :30

Announcer will call for fights by asking Brawlers to roll /random!

Announcer will close the rolls and roll /random 5 to determine who is in the matches.  Could be high rolls, low rolls, mid rolls, 2 high - 2 low, or closest to their roll.  If it is closest to their roll they will then /random and the four closest fighters will be in

Fights begin at the top of the hour, :00!

Have question feel free to reach out to a staff member!  

Now with that out of the way have a look at the different game mode rules below.
Basic Rules
8 Rounds of Attacking and Defending
Black and Blue Sides: Brawler on the Black Side attacks first by rolling /random, Brawler on the Blue Side defends by rolling /random
Roles of Attacker and Defender switch next round and so on and so forth! Brawler attacking roll is higher than the Defender, their damage is recorded. If the Defender's roll is higher no damage goes through! Brawler wins by having the higher amount of damage done to their opponent at the end of the 8 rounds!
Gauntlet Rules
 We use this for quick matches or Gauntlet Tournaments!
 Best of 5 to win the match! 
 Both Fighters roll /random during each round! Highest roll wins!
Russian Roulette
Announcer takes all fighters who have rolled to get in!
All Brawlers get in a circle, they will take turns deathrolling
The Announcer will roll /random, pass it to whoever of their choosing and that person will roll off that number.
Brawlers are allowed to roll multiple times but they must announce it first before rolling!
Battle Royale
Like Russian Roulette everyone in the ring but Announcer will call for everyone to roll /random!
Whoever has the lowest roll is out!
Final 2 Fighters do a best out of 2!
